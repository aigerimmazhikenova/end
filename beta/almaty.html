Alma-Ata is a city of republican significance and the largest settlement of the Republic of Kazakhstan, the former capital of Kazakhstan (until 1997), the Kazakh SSR (part of the USSR), the Kazakh ASSR (part of the RSFSR).

Kaindy (kaz. Kayyudy) is a lake in the Almaty region of Kazakhstan, a popular tourist destination in one of the Kungei Alatau gorges. The main attraction of the lake is the spruce trees rising straight from the water.
